What is Direct Health Alliance?

Direct Health Alliance is a comprehensive, transparent marketplace for providers, pharmacies, and other health services to direct connect with patients and employers. By connecting direct, patients and employers will see substantial cost reductions in every aspect of the healthcare experience, often up to 90%. Providers, pharmacies, and other health care services can focus exclusively on quality and access instead of spending time & money on billing, insurance negotiations, contracts, marketing, commissions to middlemen, and other non-value added parts of the healthcare system. This win/win direct relationship changes the dynamic of healthcare by restoring the doctor/patient relationship, expanding access to quality care, and ensures the vast majority of healthcare dollars are spent on value-added patient care.
